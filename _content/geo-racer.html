GeoRacer was a collaborative effort to build a real-time interactive app for the Phoenix Phrenzy contest. GeoRacer was awarded first place at Phoenix Phrenzy and a 2019 GDUSA American Graphic Design award.

The gameplay cenetered around a realtime meter that provided a clear stystem status to how close a user was to the desired location.
