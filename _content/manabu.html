Manabu is a startup working to create a brand new virtual conference expereince. The experience and flow included the ability to customize a variety of talk interactions with the audience.

We tested and explored features that provided live interactions with an audience and a remote speaker.
The tool was created to give a speaker control of their own content with the ability to choose ads and sell tickets to their presentations.
