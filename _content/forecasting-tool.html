Our client desired better undertanding of how diffrent variables would impact their business. We mapped out a system that allowed their business team to create reports in different scenarios based on a variety of business factors.

The complexity of their manufacturing processes required the ability to set variables to different lines and products.
We kept the flow and views simple in our initial phase in order to tackle the complexity of maniputlating large amounts of data.
