Our client desired better undertanding of how diffrent variables would impact their buisnees. We mapped out a system that allowed thier business team to created reports on different scenerios based on a variety of buisness factors.

The compexity of thier maufacturing processes required the abiliy to set variables to different lines and products.
We kept the flow and views simple in our initial phase in order to tackle the complexity of maniputlating large amounts of data.
